## Creating Your Account

### Authentication Options

| Method | How It Works |
|--------|--------------|
| **Magic Link** | Enter email ‚Üí receive login link ‚Üí click to authenticate |
| **Google OAuth** | Sign in with Google account |

### Steps to Register

1. Click **Login** in the navigation bar
2. Choose your preferred authentication method
3. Follow the prompts

### Setting Up Your Profile

After first login:

1. Click your avatar/name in the header
2. Select **Profile Settings**
3. Add:
   - **Display Name**: How you appear to others
   - **Bio**: Brief description (optional)
   - **Wallet Address**: For verification (optional)
   - **Notification Preferences**: Email alerts

## Writing Community Reviews

### When to Write a Review

- You have experience with or knowledge about a project
- You've researched the project and can offer informed perspective
- You want to help other community members make decisions

### How to Submit a Review

1. Navigate to any project detail page
2. Scroll to **Community Reviews** section
3. Click **Write Review** button
4. Fill out the review form:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Overall Rating** | Yes | 1-5 stars |
| **Title** | Yes | Brief summary (max 100 chars) |
| **Content** | Yes | Detailed assessment (min 50 chars) |
| **Alignment Score** | No | How well aligns with Cardano goals (1-5) |
| **Feasibility Score** | No | How realistic the plan is (1-5) |
| **Auditability Score** | No | How verifiable progress is (1-5) |

5. Click **Submit Review**
6. Your review appears publicly on the project page

### Best Practices for Reviews

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Be specific and cite evidence
- ‚úÖ Separate facts from opinions
- ‚úÖ Consider the project's scope and funding level
- ‚úÖ Acknowledge both strengths and weaknesses
- ‚úÖ Update your review if circumstances change

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Make unsubstantiated claims
- ‚ùå Use inflammatory language
- ‚ùå Review projects you have conflicts of interest with
- ‚ùå Copy/paste the same review on multiple projects
- ‚ùå Include personal attacks on team members

### Review Voting

Other users can vote your review as **Helpful** or **Not Helpful**:
- Helpful votes increase your reputation
- Reviews with many helpful votes appear first
- Consistently unhelpful reviews may be deprioritized

## Submitting Concerns

### What Is a Concern?

A concern is a structured way to flag specific issues with a project. Unlike reviews (which are general assessments), concerns are focused on particular problems.

### When to Submit a Concern

- You've identified a specific issue that others should know about
- You have evidence to support your concern
- The issue is factual, not just a matter of opinion

### Concern Categories

| Category | Use When |
|----------|----------|
| **Delayed Delivery** | Milestones are significantly behind schedule |
| **Communication Issues** | Team is unresponsive to community |
| **Quality Issues** | Deliverables don't meet expectations |
| **Misrepresentation** | Proposal claims don't match reality |
| **Other** | Issues that don't fit above categories |

### How to Submit

1. Navigate to project detail page
2. Find **Concerns** section
3. Click **Report Concern**
4. Fill out the form:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Category** | Yes | Select from dropdown |
| **Description** | Yes | Detailed explanation |
| **Evidence URL** | No | Link to supporting evidence |

5. Click **Submit**
6. Your concern becomes visible on the project page

### What Happens Next

1. **Project team notified**: They can respond directly
2. **Public visibility**: Other users can see the concern
3. **Team response**: Project leaders can explain or address
4. **Resolution**: Concerns can be marked resolved
5. **Reputation impact**: Valid concerns increase your reputation

## Building Reputation

### How Reputation Works

Your reputation score reflects your contribution quality:

| Activity | Reputation Impact |
|----------|------------------|
| Review marked helpful | +5 points |
| Review marked not helpful | -2 points |
| Concern confirmed by moderator | +10 points |
| Concern dismissed as invalid | -5 points |
| Response to your concern resolves issue | +3 points |

### Reputation Tiers

| Tier | Points | Benefits |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Bronze** | 0-99 | Basic member features |
| **Silver** | 100-499 | Reviews highlighted, faster concern processing |
| **Gold** | 500+ | Trusted contributor badge, priority support |

### Tips for Building Reputation

1. Write thoughtful, evidence-based reviews
2. Submit concerns only when you have clear evidence
3. Engage constructively with responses
4. Be consistent in your contributions
